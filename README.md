# 🚗 Ride-Hailing Dashboard - Uber Analytics Project

## 🔹 Overview
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of ride-hailing operations, covering bookings, cancellations, customer behavior, driver performance, and revenue insights. It enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify issues, and optimize operational efficiency.

---

## 🔹 Objectives
- Analyze booking performance and ride status  
- Track cancellations (customer & driver) and their reasons  
- Evaluate driver and customer behavior  
- Monitor revenue and pricing efficiency  
- Improve operational decision-making  

---

## 🔹 Dataset Includes
- Date & Time  
- Booking ID & Status  
- Customer ID  
- Vehicle Type  
- Pickup & Drop Locations  
- Avg VTAT (Vehicle Time Arrival)  
- Avg CTAT (Customer Time Arrival)  
- Cancellation Metrics (Customer & Driver + Reasons)  
- Incomplete Rides + Reasons  
- Booking Value  
- Ride Distance  
- Driver Ratings & Customer Ratings  
- Payment Method  
- Price per Kilometer  

---

## 🔹 Key Features

### 📌 Booking Analysis
- Total bookings and ride status distribution (Completed, Cancelled, Incomplete)  
- Peak booking times (daily & hourly trends)  

### ❌ Cancellation Analysis
- Customer vs Driver cancellations  
- Detailed cancellation reasons  
- Identification of high cancellation patterns  

### ⏱️ Time Performance
- Average VTAT & CTAT analysis  
- Service efficiency tracking  

### 💰 Revenue Analysis
- Total booking value  
- Price per kilometer evaluation  
- Revenue trends over time  

### 👥 Customer Insights
- Customer behavior and booking patterns  
- Customer ratings analysis  

### 🚘 Driver Performance
- Driver ratings comparison  
- Cancellation behavior by drivers  

### 🌍 Location Analysis
- Pickup & Drop hotspots  
- High-demand areas  

---

## 🔹 Filters & Interactivity
The dashboard includes dynamic filters for:
- Date & Time  
- Vehicle Type  
- Location  
- Booking Status  
- Payment Method  

All visuals are fully interactive for deep data exploration.

---

## 🔹 Tools & Technologies
- Power BI  
- Data Modeling  
- DAX  

---

## 🔹 Key Insights
- High cancellation rates can indicate operational inefficiencies  
- Certain locations show higher demand and ride concentration  
- Driver behavior directly impacts service quality  
- Peak hours significantly influence revenue generation  
- Price per kilometer varies based on demand and trip distance

## 🔹 Business Value
This dashboard helps:
- Improve customer experience  
- Reduce cancellations  
- Optimize driver allocation  
- Increase revenue efficiency
